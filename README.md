<h2>ğŸ§© Problem Background</h2>
<p>
  Prior to TalentLink, job placement processes at APU were largely
  <strong>manual and fragmented</strong>, resulting in inefficiencies and high
  administrative workload.

<ul>
  <li>Manual job postings via email and noticeboards</li>
  <li>Non-digital job applications (email / paper resumes)</li>
  <li>Ineffective screening and shortlisting</li>
  <li>Uncoordinated interview scheduling</li>
  <li>No real-time application tracking</li>
  <li>Weak data management and security</li>
</ul>
